Changing a recipe

In order to change the recipe for a dish you need:

  • Select the dish for which you want to change the recipe and click on it on the table of recipes for dishes.
  • On the right under the recipe, click on the 'Редактировать' button.
  • After that, a window will appear with a field in which there will be a recipe, which you can edit.
  • To confirm editing press OK, to cancel press Cancel.

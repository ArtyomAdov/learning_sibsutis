Sorting and filtering dishes

To sort or filter the recipe table, you need:

  • Next to 'Сортировка', click on the pull-out menu, select sorting and click on it.
  • Or, next 'Кухня', click on the pull-down menu, select a kitchen and click on it.
  • Or, next to the 'Тип блюда' inscription, click on the pull-down menu, select a dish type and click on it.
  • To reset filtering, select an empty filter from the pull-down menu next to 'Кухня' or next to 'Тип блюда'.

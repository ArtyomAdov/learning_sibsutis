Creating a dish

To create a recipe you need:

  • On the toolbar, click 'Меню', then select 'Добавить рецепт'.
  • After that, a window will appear in which you need to fill in all the fields. Enter 'Да' or nothing in the 'Избранное' field.
  • After entering the data in the fields, click OK to confirm the addition or Cancel to cancel.

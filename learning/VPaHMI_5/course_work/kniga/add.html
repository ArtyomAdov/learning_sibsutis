Adding to favorites and setting the cooking date

To add to favorites you need:

  • Select the desired dish on the recipe table and click on it.
  • On the right under the recipe, check the box labeled 'Избранное'.
  • If you uncheck the box, the reset is removed from the favorites.

To mark that you prepared a dish today you need:

  • Select the desired dish on the recipe table and click on it.
  • On the right under the recipe, check the box labeled 'Дата приготовления'.
